Looking for My Research?

We’ve renamed this to My Favorites. After signing in, use the pin icon in the upper right to access your saved items.

  • If you had items in folders, you can now filter these using “labels.”
  • If you had saved searches, please re-create them. We improved the technology that supports saved searches.
